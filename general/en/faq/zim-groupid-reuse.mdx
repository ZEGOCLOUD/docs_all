<Title>When using ZIM SDK, after dissolving a group, can I use the GroupID of this group to create a new group?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="In-app chat" platform="All" />



- - -

No, ZIM product design requires that GroupID must be unique and cannot be reused to ensure that the underlying data logic will not be confused.
