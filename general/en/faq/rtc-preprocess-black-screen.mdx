<Title>When using ZegoEffects in the custom preprocessing feature of RTC, the preview and published stream show a black screen. How to handle this?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="AI Effects" platform="iOS / Android / macOS / Windows" />



---

Do not call the `initEnv` interface in the `onCapturedUnprocessedTextureData` callback of RTC. It needs to be called in the `onStart` callback.
