<Title>How to solve Metal-related crash when debugging on iOS 12 physical device?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Roomkit" platform="iOS" />


---

If a Metal-related crash occurs when debugging on a physical device with iOS 12 system, you need to uncheck the 'API Validation' option in XCODE's [Edit Scheme] -> [Run] -> [Diagnostics] -> [Metal].
