<Title>Does the "Real-time Messaging and Signaling" feature of Video Call (ZEGO Express SDK) support querying history messages?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Video Call / Audio Call / Live streaming" platform="All" />



- - -

No. If you need to query history messages, you can integrate the [Instant Messaging (ZIM SDK)](/zim-android/introduction/overview) product, which provides functions such as sending and receiving messages, querying history messages, and deleting messages.
