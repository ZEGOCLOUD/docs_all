<Title>If the client integrates Express SDK, is it necessary to maintain the room?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Video Call / Audio Call / Live streaming" platform="All" />



- - -

Rooms are automatically created and deleted, and clients do not need to handle or maintain them. When all clients leave a room, the room is automatically deleted.
