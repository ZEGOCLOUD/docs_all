<Title>Is there an administrator in each room during a call?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Video Call / Audio Call / Live streaming" platform="All" />



- - -

There is no concept of an administrator.

The management of calls in a room is within the scope of business management layer, which can be implemented by customers through their own signaling layer, calling SDK interfaces ([startPublishingStream](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~Java_android~class~im-zego-zegoexpress-zego-express-engine&jumpType=route#start-publishing-stream)/[stopPublishingStream](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~Java_android~class~im-zego-zegoexpress-zego-express-engine&jumpType=route#stop-publishing-stream)/[startPlayingStream](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~Java_android~class~im-zego-zegoexpress-zego-express-engine&jumpType=route#start-playing-stream)/[stopPlayingStream](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~Java_android~class~im-zego-zegoexpress-zego-express-engine&jumpType=route#stop-playing-stream)) to implement call management. Or use the SDK's signaling service for related notifications. For details, please refer to [Real-time Messaging and Signaling](https://doc-zh.zego.im/real-time-video-android-java/room/messaging-and-signaling).
