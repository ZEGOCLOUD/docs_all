<Title>Web platform uses "https" to go online, but the flv play stream address returned by SDK is "http", which is incompatible. How to solve?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Video Call / Audio Call / Live streaming" platform="Web" />



- - -

Please contact ZEGOCLOUD Technical Support and provide the "https" certificate for the play stream domain. After ZEGOCLOUD Technical Support configures the certificate, you can use "https" to pull the flv address.
