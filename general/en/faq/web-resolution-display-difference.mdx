<Title>On the Web platform, when the set video resolution "width" is greater than "height", how is it displayed on PC and mobile respectively?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Video Call / Live streaming" platform="Web" />



- - - 

When the set video resolution "width" is greater than "height", for example 1280*720, it is displayed as `landscape` on PC and `portrait` on mobile.
