<Title>Why does the whiteboard window become smaller and appear abnormal in the recording result?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Cloud recording" platform="All" />


---

If the ratio of `HorizontalRatio` and `VerticalRatio` of `Whiteboard` is inconsistent with the aspect ratio of the whiteboard used by the user's client, it will cause the recorded whiteboard to display abnormally (the whiteboard area will be very small).
