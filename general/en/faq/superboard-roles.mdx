<Title>Does Super Board distinguish between roles such as teachers and students?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Super Board" platform="All" />


---

No, roles are part of business logic and need to be implemented by developers. ZEGO only provides basic whiteboard capabilities.

Developers can set client permissions through ``setOperationMode`` and combine with role business logic to implement different permissions for different roles.
