<Title>How to handle the issue where H5 cannot see the teacher's video in Web large class?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Roomkit" platform="Web" />


---

When encountering this problem, please first confirm with technical support whether the stream mixing service has been enabled. If it has not been enabled, you need to apply for activation.
If you confirm that the stream mixing service has been enabled, please refer to [Web Large Class, Cannot See Teacher's Video When Joining as a Student](/faq/h5-teacher-video-missing) for troubleshooting.
