<Title>Why does the SDK crash on macOS 10.13 and below system versions?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Screen sharing" platform="macOS" />


---

Version 2.8 or earlier may have this issue due to not supporting the xplatform base library.

Version 2.9 and later have resolved this issue. For the latest SDK package, please contact ZEGOCLOUD Technical Support.
