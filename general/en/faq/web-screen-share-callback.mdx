<Title>On the Web platform, during screen sharing, when clicking the "Stop Sharing" button, will there be a callback message?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Video Call" platform="Web" />



- - -

Developers can call the [on](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~javascript_web~class~ZegoExpressEngine#on) interface's [screenSharingEnded](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~javascript_web~interface~ZegoRTCEvent#screen-sharing-ended) callback to listen for callback information related to stopping screen sharing.

For details, please refer to [Screen Sharing](https://doc-zh.zego.im/real-time-video-web/video/screen-sharing).
