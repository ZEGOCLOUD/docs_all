<Title>What are the alternative solutions after RoomKit and Go Classroom products and their related solutions are delisted?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Call Kit" platform="iOS / Android" />



As of May 31, 2024, **RoomKit** and **Go Classroom** products and their related solutions have been delisted from the official website. ZEGOCLOUD provides [UIKits](https://www.zegocloud.com/uikits) products, which are audio and video SDKs with standardized UI, to help you quickly implement scenarios such as small classes and large classes.

## UIKits Product Introduction

UIKits is based on scenario-based best practices, integrating basic products such as RTC, ZIM, and AI Effects filters, providing audio and video SDKs with standardized UI to help developers easily build interactive scenarios such as calls, live streaming, and voice chat with less than ten lines of code, accelerating product launch. For more details, please refer to [UIKits](https://www.zegocloud.com/uikits).

Product Features:
- Diverse application definitions
  - 5+ SDKs with UI scenarios, including audio and video calls, live streaming, voice chat rooms, etc.
  - 50+ UI components, supporting custom configuration and providing advanced capabilities such as beauty filters.
  - 7+ platforms and frameworks, Android, iOS, Web, uni-app, Flutter, etc.
- Convenient integration and access
  - Quick to get started, lowering the learning threshold.
  - Out-of-the-box, build applications with less than 10 lines of code.
  - Fully open source, UI components and business code are fully open source.
