<Title>How to specify StreamID for cloud recording?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Cloud recording" platform="All" />



- - -

There are the following two cases:

- Single stream recording: Specify the StreamId of the stream to be recorded by setting the StreamId parameter under the `RecordStreamList` member.
- Mixed stream recording: Specify the StreamId of the stream to be recorded in the screen by setting the StreamId parameter under the `MixInputList` member.

For details, please refer to [Start Recording](/cloud-recording-server/start).
