<Title>How many streams can a user pull simultaneously at most?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Video Call / Audio Call / Live streaming" platform="iOS / Android / macOS / Windows" />



- - -

The SDK supports 50 concurrent play streams by default, and the runtime will be set to 12 play streams by default. When developers need to support more than 12 play streams, they need to contact ZEGOCLOUD Technical Support for configuration.
