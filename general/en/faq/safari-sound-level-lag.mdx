<Title>When enabling the sound level feature during mixed streaming, playing video in Safari browser shows lag. How to handle this?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Video Call / Audio Call / Live streaming" platform="iOS" />



- - -

### Problem Cause

If the sound level "soundLevel" is enabled during mixed streaming, the sound level will be in "SEI" format, which is not compatible with Safari browser.

### Solution

Please set the parameter "withSoundLevel" to "false" to disable the mixed stream sound level feature.
