- - -

ZEGO is a global cloud communication service provider that provides comprehensive audio and video services globally. Its products cover key areas such as live streaming, social, gaming, finance, healthcare, government, conferences, education, call centers, and smart IoT.

Enterprises or developers can easily obtain real-time audio and video communication capabilities by integrating the SDK provided by ZEGO. By calling APIs, you can implement Video Call, live streaming, interactive whiteboard, file sharing, recording, and other functions.
