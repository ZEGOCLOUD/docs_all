<Title>How to monitor who is speaking in the room with Express SDK?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Video Call / Audio Call / Live streaming" platform="All" />



---

- In the case of playing a single stream, after playing the stream successfully, call [startSoundLevelMonitor](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~Java_android~class~im-zego-zegoexpress-zego-express-engine&jumpType=route#start-sound-level-monitor) and listen to the [onRemoteSoundLevelUpdate](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~Java_android~class~im-zego-zegoexpress-callback-i-zego-event-handler&jumpType=route#on-remote-sound-level-update) callback to get the volume information.
- In the case of playing a mixed stream, first start the sound level option in the mixed stream configuration (i.e. [enableSoundLevel](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~Java_android~class~im-zego-zegoexpress-entity-zego-mixer-task&jumpType=route#enable-sound-level) set to "true"), and specify a unique "soundLevelID" for each input stream, then listen to the [onMixerSoundLevelUpdate](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~ObjectiveC_ios~protocol~zego-event-handler&jumpType=route#on-mixer-sound-level-update) callback to get the volume information.
