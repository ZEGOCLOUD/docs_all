<Title>In mixed stream recording with custom layout, if mix_input_list has only one layout, can multiple streams be recorded?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Cloud recording" platform="All" />


---

No. In this case, only the first stream will be recorded, and subsequent streams will not be recorded.
