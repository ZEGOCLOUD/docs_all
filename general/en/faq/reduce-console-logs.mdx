<Title>How to reduce the information printed by Express SDK in the Web console?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="Video Call / Audio Call / Live streaming" platform="Web" />



- - -


Call the [setLogConfig](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~javascript_web~class~ZegoExpressEngine#set-log-config) interface and set the log level printed in the local console through the "logLevel" property in the [ZegoLogConfig](https://docs.zegocloud.com/article/api?doc=Express_Video_SDK_API~javascript_web~interface~ZegoLogConfig) object.


In general, ZEGOCLOUD does not recommend setting the log level too high. Keep the default value `info`. The higher the log level is set, the less logs are printed, making it difficult to troubleshoot when problems occur.
