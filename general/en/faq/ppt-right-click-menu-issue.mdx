<Title>Why doesn't a menu appear when right-clicking on the transcoded dynamic PPT file page?</Title>

<ArticleMetadata language="en" product="File sharing / Super Board" platform="Web" />


----

For the security of shared files, to prevent users from directly downloading resources (videos, images) from PPT through the right-click menu when sharing dynamic PPT files, ZEGO has processed the transcoding of dynamic PPT. The transcoded files have disabled the right-click menu, and non-dynamic PPT transcoding is not affected.

In addition, if developers also want to prevent users from downloading images in dynamic PPT through copying or dragging, we also provide the DisableH5ImageDrag property to disable the copy and drag effects of images in dynamic PPT.
