If the console reports an error similar to the one shown in the image above when calling SDK interfaces, it may be caused by compiling and packaging the SDK.

It is recommended to refer to the [Integration](/faq/roomkit-go-class-alternatives) document to introduce the SDK through the script method without compiling and packaging the SDK.
