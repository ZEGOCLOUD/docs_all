# ZEGO Digital Human Service Privacy Policy

**Version Updated: September 10, 2024**

Shenzhen ZEGO Technology Co., Ltd. and its affiliates (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "ZEGO"), as the operators of [ZEGO Digital Human Service], deeply understand the importance of personal information to users. We will protect users' personal information and privacy security in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. We have developed this Privacy Policy and wish to inform individual or enterprise developer users (hereinafter referred to as "you") of [ZEGO Digital Human Service] of the following content to enable you to make an informed choice.

The ZEGO Digital Human Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "this Privacy Policy") will explain to you how [ZEGO Digital Human Service] will collect, use, and store your personal information and what rights you are entitled to. Please carefully read and thoroughly understand this Privacy Policy before using any [ZEGO Digital Human Service], and only begin using it after confirming that you fully understand and agree to it. Please note that we periodically review and update our privacy policy, so the related measures may change accordingly. We kindly ask you to visit this page regularly to ensure you stay informed about the latest version of our Privacy Policy.

Your use or continued use of [ZEGO Digital Human Service] indicates your consent to our collection, use, storage, and sharing of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
