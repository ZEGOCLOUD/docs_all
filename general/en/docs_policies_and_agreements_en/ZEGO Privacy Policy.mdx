## ZEGO Cloud Service Privacy Policy

**Version Updated: August 26, 2024**

Thank you for using ZEGO Cloud Services. We understand the importance of your personal information and will make every effort to protect its security.

This Privacy Policy is designed to help developers (hereinafter referred to as "you") understand what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, how we protect it, and how you can manage and control your own information.

**Before registering, browsing, or using ZEGO Cloud Services, please confirm that you have carefully read, fully understood, and accepted the terms of this policy (minors should read this under the supervision of a legal guardian), particularly the terms that limit or exclude liability.**

If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions, we are happy to hear from you. Please feel free to contact us through the contact information provided in this Privacy Policy, and we will address your inquiries.

We have also prepared an SDK Privacy Policy to explain to developers (or potential developers) and end users who use our SDK products and services: in order to ensure the availability of SDK functions, we will collect, use, and protect end users' personal information. Before integrating and using our products, developers (or potential developers) and their end users may refer to the [ZEGO SDK Privacy Policy](./ZEGO%20SDK%20Privacy%20Policy.mdx) for details.

### 1. Definition of Information

- "Information" in this Privacy Policy refers specifically to the corresponding data mentioned in **"2. Scope of Information We Collect"**. This Privacy Policy does not apply to data you store through ZEGO Cloud Services.

- "ZEGO Cloud Services" in this Privacy Policy refers to all products and services provided on the ZEGO website, including but not limited to real-time audio and video, real-time messaging, interactive whiteboard, and cloud recording.

### 2. Scope of Information We Collect

**When you use our products and services as a developer (or potential developer), we will collect the following data from you in the following scenarios:**

- When you register and log in to ZEGO Cloud Services, we will collect your personal information based on your chosen registration and login method:
  - Phone number/verification code registration and login scenario: phone number;
  - Password login scenario: login credentials;
  - Email/verification code login scenario: email address;
  - WeChat registration and login scenario: WeChat nickname, WeChat account.

- When you choose the following scenarios for personal identity verification, we will collect your personal information:
  - Facial recognition verification scenario: name, ID number;
  - Phone number verification scenario: name, ID number, phone number;
  - Bank card verification scenario: document type, name, document number (depending on your chosen document type, this may be an ID number, Mainland Travel Permit for Hong Kong and Macau Residents, Taiwan Compatriot Permit number, or passport number), personal bank card number, bank card registered phone number.

- When you conduct enterprise identity verification, we will collect your personal information: company name, industry/scenario, document type, document number (depending on your chosen document type, this may be: Unified Social Credit Code, Organization Code, Business Registration Number, Business Registration Certificate, Tax Registration Certificate, Legal Entity Code Certificate, Public Institution Legal Entity Certificate, Social Organization Registration Certificate, Private Non-Enterprise Institution Registration Certificate, Registration Certificate for Permanent Representative Office of Foreign Organizations, Government Approval Document, or Company Number (overseas)), province/city, street address, company phone number, invoice information (taxpayer identification number, bank account, opening bank), billing information (billing receiving email).

- When you apply for invoice issuance, we will collect your personal information: invoice information (invoice title type, invoice type, invoice title, taxpayer identification number, opening bank, opening account, enterprise address, enterprise phone number), mailing information (recipient name, contact phone number, mailing address).

- When you apply to sign an electronic contract, we will collect your personal information: signing information (signing type, signing entity), contact person name, contact address, contact phone number, email address, billing email, invoice information (invoice type, taxpayer identification number, opening bank, bank account, company address).

- When you submit a support ticket, we will collect your personal information: phone number, name, company name, APPID.

- When you communicate with online customer service, we will collect your personal information: phone number, conversation content (including text, documents, images, audio and video you input).

- When you apply to trial our products or consult about solutions, we will collect your personal information: name, company name, phone number, email address.

- When you add login and console access permissions for other users, we will collect your personal information: name, email address, login password.

- When you need offline payment and invoice application, to fulfill contractual obligations, we will collect your personal information: invoice title type, invoice type, invoice title, taxpayer identification number, primary opening bank, primary opening account, enterprise registered address, enterprise registered phone number, recipient name, recipient phone number, recipient email, detailed mailing address.

- When you use the console, based on website and service operation needs, we will also collect the following personal information:
  - Network information: including IP address, carrier, website browsing history;
  - Device information: website click records, operating system, hardware type, hardware model, browser type, device ID, browser language, layout engines;
  - User identification codes: SID, CID, UID, APPID;
  - Log information.

### 3. How We Collect Information

- We collect your information through your voluntary submissions and through records generated during your use of our services.

- We may also collect and use your information through Cookies and other related technologies. Our specific uses of Cookies include:
  - Remembering your identity and authentication status, for example: Cookies help us identify you as a registered user and determine whether you are logged in and authenticated.
  - Improving your browsing experience, for example: providing services such as your preferred language, time zone, region, and font.

- You may refuse or manage Cookies through your browser settings. Please note that if you disable Cookies, you may not be able to enjoy the optimal service experience, and the availability of certain features may be affected.

- Information recorded through ZEGO Cloud Services Cookies is subject to this Privacy Policy.

For example, we may use your information for identity verification, security protection, complaint handling, dispute resolution, fraud monitoring, and other purposes; or, when you use security features or other similar services, we may detect malicious programs or viruses, or identify fraudulent information for you.

- Other relevant scenarios that may require the use of collected information. If the use scenario has no reasonable connection to the original scenario, we will obtain your consent again before using the information.

ZEGO Cloud Services accounts have security protection features, and you also need to protect your personal information properly. For cases where children's personal information is collected with parental consent, we will only use or publicly disclose such information when permitted by law, with the explicit consent of a parent or guardian, or when necessary to protect children. ### 10. Your Rights

- You can access, modify, and delete your relevant information during your use of our services. The methods for accessing, modifying, and deleting information will depend on the specific service you use.

- If you find that the information we have collected and stored about you contains errors and you cannot correct them yourself, you may also request us to make corrections.

- When accessing, modifying, and deleting relevant information, we may require you to undergo identity verification to protect account security. Please understand that due to technical limitations and legal requirements, some of your requests may not be fulfilled.

- In the following circumstances, you may submit a request to us for the deletion of your personal information:
  - If our processing of personal information violates laws and regulations.
  - If we collect and use your personal information without obtaining your consent.
  - If our processing of personal information violates our agreement with you.
  - If you no longer use our products or services, or you have deactivated your account.
  - If we no longer provide products or services to you.

If we decide to respond to your deletion request, we will also notify entities that have obtained your personal information from us and require them to promptly delete it, unless otherwise required by laws and regulations or these entities have obtained your independent authorization. After you delete information from our services, we may not immediately delete the corresponding information from backup systems, but we will delete it when backups are updated.

- Due to lawful requirements, information security protection, and other legitimate reasons, some of your information may not be accessible, modifiable, or deletable.

- You may choose to withdraw your consent to the processing of your information for certain non-essential features or services, and choose to deactivate your ZEGO Cloud Services account by contacting customer service.

- If you have questions about exercising the above rights, you may contact us using the relevant contact information provided in **"12. Contact Us"**.
