# Integrity and Compliance, Safety, and Illegal Activity Reporting Guide

## Integrity and Compliance Reporting Guide

ZEGO is committed to fostering an open, transparent, and fair business ecosystem in its business relationships with customers, business partners, and other relevant parties. We firmly crack down on any corrupt behavior. To uphold this commitment, all ZEGO employees comply with all applicable laws and regulations as well as the company's internal Code of Conduct in business activities. The support of our partners is very important to ZEGO. If you wish to report any suspected unethical or illegal behavior by ZEGO employees, please feel free to contact the ZEGO Integrity and Compliance Department at any time.

The ZEGO Integrity and Compliance Department accepts reports involving the following employee misconduct:

1. Soliciting or accepting bribes; accepting gifts or entertainment in violation of regulations;
2. Investing in or working for ZEGO's suppliers, merchants, or partners in violation of regulations;
3. Abusing authority for personal gain;
4. Stealing, leaking, selling, or exploiting confidential company information;
5. Gross negligence causing serious impact to the company;
6. Other violations of laws and regulations.

The ZEGO Integrity and Compliance Department encourages you to file reports using your real identity or provide contact information so that a dedicated person can maintain communication with you. We commit to strictly protecting whistleblowers and keeping the content of reports strictly confidential. At the same time, please confirm that the reported content must be objective and truthful. We reserve the right to pursue legal liability against those who make malicious false accusations. For reports that are formally investigated and concluded, we will provide feedback based on the contact information you have provided.

## Safety and Illegal Activity Reporting Guide

To create a safe, lawful, and compliant online environment and business atmosphere, and to protect the legitimate rights and interests of users and the public, ZEGO has established a dedicated safety and illegal activity reporting channel, encouraging everyone to actively report various safety hazards, infringements of user rights, and illegal activities. This Guide provides detailed information on reporting-related matters.

### I. Reportable Content

#### (I) Safety-Related

1. **Website Security Vulnerabilities**: Issues such as SQL injection vulnerabilities, cross-site scripting (XSS) vulnerabilities, file upload vulnerabilities, and other security risks on company websites that could lead to user information leakage, website tampering, etc.
2. **Data Security Hazards**: Including but not limited to unencrypted data during internal storage and transmission, suspected data leakage incidents, such as the discovery of company user data or trade secret data through unofficial channels.
3. **Infringement of Personal Information Rights**:
    - **Illegal Collection of Personal Information**: Collecting users' names, ID numbers, contact information, addresses, account passwords, financial status, location tracking, and other sensitive personal information without the user's knowledge or explicit consent.
    - **Unauthorized Use of Personal Information**: Using collected personal information for purposes inconsistent with the original collection purpose without user authorization.
    - **Improper Storage of Personal Information**: The company's storage methods for personal information have obvious security deficiencies that could easily lead to information leakage.
    - **Leakage of Personal Information**: User personal information is leaked externally due to poor internal management, technical vulnerabilities, or other reasons.
4. **Algorithm Safety Issues**:
    - **Algorithmic Discrimination**: Algorithms produce unfair differential treatment toward specific groups (e.g., based on gender, age, race, region, etc.) during data processing and decision-making.
    - **Algorithmic Price Discrimination**: Using algorithms to set differentiated prices based on users' consumption habits, purchasing power, etc., for existing or specific user groups, harming user rights and interests.
    - **"Information Cocoon" Effect**: Algorithms continuously push content similar to users' existing interest preferences through personalized recommendations, causing users' exposure to information to become increasingly narrow, making it difficult to access diverse perspectives and information, and limiting users' cognition and horizons.
    - **Algorithmic Manipulation and Abuse**: The company uses algorithms to deliberately manipulate search results, recommendation rankings, trending lists, etc., misleading users and interfering with normal market competition and information dissemination order.

#### (II) Infringement of Minors' Rights

1. **Violation of Minors' Privacy**: The company's website or related products/services collect, use, or disclose minors' names, ID numbers, contact information, home addresses, photos, videos, and other personal privacy information without the consent of minors and their guardians; conducting "doxxing" against minors or maliciously spreading and leaking minors' privacy content.
2. **Misleading Inappropriate Content**: Products contain content that promotes unhealthy behaviors and habits, spreads harmful values, or induces dangerous behavior.
3. **Inducing Minors' Consumption**: Exploiting minors' psychological characteristics through false advertising, misleading language, and other means to encourage excessive consumption by minors, such as urging minors to purchase high-priced virtual items or blind boxes.
4. **Absence or Failure of Online Protection Mechanisms for Minors**: The company's products or services claim to have minor protection features (such as youth mode, anti-addiction systems, etc.), but are not effectively implemented or have vulnerabilities.

#### (III) Illegal Information and Activities

1. **Intellectual Property Infringement**: The company's website contains content that uses others' trademarks, patents, copyrights, and other intellectual property without authorization, such as using unauthorized images, software code, articles, etc.
2. **Dissemination of Illegal Information**: Including but not limited to promoting terrorism, extremism, inciting secession, undermining national unity, endangering national security, honor, and interests; disseminating violent or pornographic information; fabricating and spreading false information that disrupts economic and social order.
