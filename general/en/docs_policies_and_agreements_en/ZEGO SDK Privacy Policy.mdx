**Version Updated: August 15, 2025**

**Version Effective Date: August 15, 2025**


Thank you for using the SDK products and/or services (hereinafter referred to as "SDK Products") provided by Shenzhen ZEGO Technology Co., Ltd. and its affiliates (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "ZEGO"). As the provider of SDK Products, we attach great importance to personal information protection.

This Privacy Policy is designed to help mobile application developers (hereinafter referred to as "developers") and their end users (hereinafter referred to as "you") understand what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, how we protect it, and how you can manage and control your own information.

After developers integrate our SDK into their developed and/or operated websites or application software (including apps, mini-programs, web pages, etc., hereinafter referred to as "Developer Applications"), they can provide end users with the SDK-related functions and services described in this Privacy Policy through the Developer Applications. When our SDK is integrated into Developer Applications, we are entrusted with processing the Developer Applications' relevant data information, which may include the personal information of end users of the Developer Applications. We are deeply aware of our responsibility to process your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws and regulations, and to protect your personal information and privacy security.

Special Declaration for Developers:

 - **Before integrating and using our SDK products and/or services, developers should provide this Privacy Policy to users of the final product so that end users can understand the relevant content of this Privacy Policy.**

 - Developers must ensure that our SDK products are integrated and end users' personal information is processed only after obtaining end users' consent.

 - Once developers begin using our SDK products, it indicates that they have fully understood and agreed to this Privacy Policy, and consented to our collection, use, storage, and sharing of relevant information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If developers have any questions, comments, or suggestions regarding the content of this Privacy Policy, they may contact us through the contact information provided in this Policy.

Special Declaration for End Users:

 - Developers should comply with applicable laws, policies, and regulations related to personal information protection, protect the personal information security of end users, and disclose their privacy policy to you through their applications to declare how they collect, process, and protect your personal information.

 - Developers, as "personal information processors," determine the purpose and method of processing user data. In the process of providing SDK product-related functions to developers, we only collect data on behalf of developers and process data in accordance with developers' entrustment and instructions.

 - If you need to access your personal information, correct or delete incorrect information, or no longer wish to use applications integrated with our SDK products, please contact the relevant developer (personal information processor) directly through the contact information publicly disclosed in the Developer Application.

 - The services you use through Developer Applications are independently configured by developers based on their application needs. Depending on the SDK version integrated by developers, service functionality and personal information processing may vary.

### 1. How We Collect and Use Personal Information

- To help developers provide end users with the corresponding functions and services of SDK (Software Development Kit) products, we will collect personal information generated by end users when using functions related to SDK products.

- To ensure that end users can normally use the corresponding functions and services of SDK products, **we will apply for the following system device permissions of end users' devices through the Developer Application, and will seek end users' consent before applying. End users may choose "Allow" or "Deny" permission requests.** After authorization by end users, we will enable the relevant permissions. End users may cancel authorization in the system at any time based on the SDK version integrated in the Developer Application, referring to the device and operating system's relevant instructions or guidelines. Canceling authorization will result in end users being unable to use the relevant business functions, but will not prevent end users from using other business functions.

#### 1.8 Cloud Real-Time Speech Recognition

ZEGO Cloud Real-Time Speech Recognition (hereinafter referred to as "Cloud Recognition") converts speech content in real-time audio and video scenarios such as voice calls, video live streaming, and online meetings into text results in real-time. It enables scenarios such as 1-on-1 voice call real-time subtitles and translation, online meeting real-time subtitles and post-meeting summary, global live streaming subtitles, and live room real-time host content summary.

The Cloud Real-Time Speech Recognition service is an add-on service built on ZEGO's Express SDK real-time audio and video service. It does not directly collect end user personal information itself. After developers integrate the Cloud Real-Time Speech Recognition service, some personal information collected by Express SDK (conversation information, real-time call information) will be transmitted to the Cloud Real-Time Speech Recognition service to implement the function of converting speech content into text results. At the same time, the conversation information and real-time call information collected by Express SDK will also be transmitted to third-party speech recognition API services for processing, so that speech content can be more accurately and efficiently converted into text content in real-time. For details on sharing user personal information with third parties, see section <a href="#3-how-we-share-transfer-and-publicly-disclose-personal-information" style={{color:"#2277ff" ,borderBottom: "none"}}>3.
