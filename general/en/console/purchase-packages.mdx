# How to purchase packages

- - -


## Introduction
A package is the combination of a series of products and services with discounts provided by ZEGOCLOUD to developers. You can select and purchase proper packages, and ZEGOCLOUD will automatically deduct the usage included in the package when the fee is settled.

If you are interested in packages provided by ZEGOCLOUD after browsing on the website or in the ZEGOCLOUD Admin Console, you can purchase packages on the [official website\|_blank](https://www.zegocloud.com/activity/special-packages?contact_source=pricing) or in the [ZEGOCLOUD Admin Console\|_blank](https://console.zegocloud.com/package/PackageSubscribe). In addition, if you want to continue using ZEGOCLOUD services when you are using the trial account or the trial account expires, you can choose to purchase a package for account upgrade.

## Procedure

### Purchase in the ZEGOCLOUD Admin Console
1. Log in to the ZEGOCLOUD Admin Console. In the navigation pane on the homepage, choose **Package > Package Subscription**.
<Frame width="auto" height="auto">
  <img src="https://doc-media.zego.im/sdk-doc/Pics/A_AdminConsole/Packages/PurchaseOnAdmin1.png" />
</Frame>


2. On the page that appears, select the package you need and click **Start Plan** for purchase.
<Frame width="auto" height="auto">
  <img src="https://doc-media.zego.im/sdk-doc/Pics/A_AdminConsole/Packages/PurchaseOnAdmin2.png" />
</Frame>

3. On the Check Order page, click **Pay now** to complete the payment. After the payment is completed, you can view the purchased package on the **Package Query** page.
<Frame width="auto" height="auto">
  <img src="https://doc-media.zego.im/sdk-doc/Pics/A_AdminConsole/Packages/PurchaseOnAdmin3.png" />
</Frame>
<Frame width="auto" height="auto">
  <img src="https://doc-media.zego.im/sdk-doc/Pics/A_AdminConsole/Packages/PurchaseOnAdmin33.png" />
</Frame>

### Purchase on the official website
1. Access the [pricing page\|_blank](https://www.zegocloud.com/pricing) (or directly access the [package purchase page on the official website\|_blank](https://www.zegocloud.com/activity/special-packages?contact_source=pricing)).
<Frame width="auto" height="auto">
  <img src="https://doc-media.zego.im/sdk-doc/Pics/A_AdminConsole/Packages/PurchaseOnOfficial1.png" />
</Frame>

2. Scroll down on the page and click **Learn more** to view all packages currently available.
<Frame width="auto" height="auto">
  <img src="https://doc-media.zego.im/sdk-doc/Pics/A_AdminConsole/Packages/PurchaseOnOfficial2.png" />
</Frame>

3. Select the package you need and click **Buy now** for purchase.
<Frame width="auto" height="auto">
  <img src="https://doc-media.zego.im/sdk-doc/Pics/A_AdminConsole/Packages/PurchaseOnOfficial3.png" />
</Frame>

4. If you have not registered or logged in, register an account or log in before you complete the payment. If you have logged in, directly go to the Check Order page for payment. Subsequent steps are the same as those for purchasing in the ZEGOCLOUD Admin Console.

### Purchase package for account upgrade
1. If you are still using the **trial account** in the ZEGOCLOUD Admin Console, you can choose to upgrade your account by purchasing a one-time package. In this case, click **Upgrade** in the bottom-left corner of the homepage.
<Frame width="auto" height="auto">
  <img src="https://doc-media.zego.im/sdk-doc/Pics/A_AdminConsole/Packages/PurchaseUpgrade1.png" />
</Frame>

<Note title="Note">

If you purchase packages in other ways, your account is also upgraded.
</Note>



2. Click **One-time Purchase** to upgrade to a standard account by purchasing a package. Once the purchase is successful, you can enjoy more user benefits.
<Frame width="auto" height="auto">
  <img src="https://doc-media.zego.im/sdk-doc/Pics/A_AdminConsole/Packages/PurchaseUpgrade2.png" />
</Frame>

3. On the package selection page that appears, complete the purchase process by referring to the process of [purchase in the ZEGOCLOUD Admin Console](#purchase-in-the-zegocloud-admin-console).
