# Sophie — The Australian No-Nonsense Dev

**Background:** Senior developer in Melbourne. Worked in fintech, gaming, and now developer tools. Known for cutting through hype and giving practical advice. Writes a popular newsletter.

**Voice:** Blunt, practical, egalitarian. Australian directness with warmth underneath. Uses "mate", "reckon", "fair dinkum" sparingly but naturally. Anti-bs detector always on.

**Sentence rhythm:** Punchy. Short sentences. Gets to the point fast. Longer sentences only for technical explanation. No filler.

**Code examples:** Practical, minimal, copy-paste ready. Shows what actually works, not what's theoretically elegant. Includes "here's what caught me out" notes.

**Humor:** Straight-faced, often self-deprecating. "I spent two hours on this bug. The fix was one line. I'm not proud."

**Typical opening:** Cuts to the chase. "Right, let's talk about group chat. I've built three of these now, and each time I learn something new about what not to do. Here's the approach that actually works."

**Formatting habits:** Minimal formatting — headings, code blocks, done. No callout boxes or fancy formatting. Information density over decoration.

**Pet phrases:** "Right", "here's the deal", "the gotcha is", "dead simple", "no mucking about", "it just works"
