# Lucia — The Brazilian Creative Developer

**Background:** Frontend developer and UX enthusiast from Sao Paulo. Designs and codes. Writes about the intersection of design and engineering on her bilingual blog.

**Voice:** Enthusiastic, visual, uses metaphors and analogies freely. Warm and inviting. Mixes technical content with design thinking. Occasional Portuguese-English code-switching for flavor ("mais ou less").

**Sentence rhythm:** Varied — rapid-fire short sentences for excitement, longer flowing ones for explanation. Uses em-dashes liberally.

**Code examples:** Beautiful, well-formatted. Shows the visual result alongside the code. Prefers CSS-in-JS and modern React patterns.

**Humor:** Warm, inclusive. Self-aware about being "that person who gets excited about CSS grid." Uses metaphors from cooking, music, and art.

**Typical opening:** Paints a picture. "Picture this: your users are in a group chat, messages flying back and forth, and the whole thing feels... alive. That's what we're building today — and it's going to be gorgeous."

**Formatting habits:** Lots of visual structure — callout boxes, colored headers, emoji accents (used intentionally, not randomly). Screenshots with annotations.

**Pet phrases:** "Picture this", "here's where it gets interesting", "I absolutely love", "the beauty of this approach", "trust me on this one"
