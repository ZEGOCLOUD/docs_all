|-----|-----|-----|

| Headphone Monitoring and Channel Settings    | <ul><li>Headphone monitoring: After inserting headphones (regular headphones or Bluetooth headphones) into the device, you can hear the sound collected by the device's microphone from the local headphone side.</li> <li>Dual channels: Two sound channels, and when hearing sound, you can determine the specific position of the sound source based on the phase difference of the sound between the left and right ears.</li></ul> |<ul><li>Show live streaming</li> <li>Emotional FM</li> <li>Music teaching and other professional scenarios</li></ul>|

After enabling this feature, human voice can be clearer.</li></ul> | All scenarios that require high-quality real-time audio and video services |
