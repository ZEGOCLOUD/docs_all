| Audio 3A Processing   | During real-time audio and video calls or live streaming, 3A processing can be performed on audio to improve call or live streaming quality and user experience.<ul><li>AEC (Acoustic Echo Cancellation): Filter captured audio data to reduce echoes in the audio.</li><li>AGC (Automatic Gain Control): After enabling this function, the SDK can automatically adjust the microphone volume, adapt to near and far sound pickup, and keep the volume stable.</li><li>ANS (Automatic Noise Suppression): Identify background noise in the sound and eliminate it. After enabling this function, the human voice can be clearer.</li></ul> | All scenarios that require high-quality real-time audio and video services |

## Live Streaming Capabilities

### Basic Features

|-----|-----|-----|
| [Stream Mixing](/real-time-video-rn/live-streaming/stream-mixing)      | Mix multiple video streams from multiple people into one stream, so that you only need to pull one stream to see the images of all members in the room and hear the sounds of all members in the room. | Multi-person call host co-hosting  |
