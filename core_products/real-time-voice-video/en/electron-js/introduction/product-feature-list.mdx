### Basic Features

| Basic Features-20%    | Feature Description-50%    | Business Scenarios-30%    |
|-----|-----|-----|
| [Audio/Video Call](/real-time-video-electron-js/quick-start/implementing-video-call)   | Users join the same room and conduct audio and video calls. | <ul><li>1v1 video call</li>  <li>Multi-person video conference</li></ul> |

| Network Speed Test | Before users publish/play streams, detect uplink and downlink network speeds to determine what bitrate of audio and video streams is suitable for publishing/playing under current network conditions.  | Call scenarios, education scenarios, live streaming scenarios  |
