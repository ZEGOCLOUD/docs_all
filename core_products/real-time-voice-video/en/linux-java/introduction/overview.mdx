# Overview

---

ZEGOCLOUD Video Call is a real-time audio and video interactive service product that provides developers with easy integration, high reliability, and cross-platform audio and video services. With an end-to-end average latency as low as 200 ms, industry-leading QoS strategies for weak network quality assurance, and powerful 3A processing capabilities, it perfectly supports one-to-many and many-to-many real-time audio and video calls, live streaming, conferences, and other scenarios.

ZEGOCLOUD Video Call also provides developers with the ability to integrate audio and video services across all platforms with just 4 lines of code, enabling developers to build products and services with perfect audio and video experiences within 30 minutes.

For related concepts, please refer to [Glossary](/glossary/term-explanation).

## Product Advantages

#### Intelligent and Realistic Video Quality

- Deep Device Adaptation: Deeply adapted to various online cameras, achieving extremely low capture latency and high video quality.
- Ultra-High Quality: Supports ultra-high quality video up to 4K 60 FPS.
- Self-Developed Encoder: Self-developed optimized codec z264 provides higher video quality under the same conditions, with extremely significant improvements for complex scenes.
- AI Video Engine: Self-developed AI video engine supports multi-frame super-resolution, low-light enhancement, background segmentation, frame interpolation, and other capabilities to comprehensively improve video quality.

#### Immersive Audio Quality

- Deep Device Adaptation: Deeply adapted to various microphones, sound cards, and other devices, achieving low-latency capture, low-latency ear return, and ultra-high fidelity audio quality.
- Rich Sound Effects: Optimized by professional audio teams, with over 30 sound effect scenarios including voice changing, voice beautification, reverb, and virtual stereo.
- 3A and Scenario-Based AI Noise Reduction: Self-developed 3A algorithm and the industry's most lightweight AI noise reduction achieve pure human voice after noise and echo suppression with extremely low performance loss. Industry-first scenario-based AI noise reduction recognizes scenarios in real-time and intelligently ensures the comprehensive effect of noise reduction and audio quality.
- Range Audio and Video: Combining distance, direction, transmission medium, and more, professional algorithms simulate realistic immersive audio and video call experiences.

#### Unlimited Possibilities and Scenarios

- Large-Scale Interactive Live Streaming: No limit on the number of people in a room, supporting up to 10,000 people in audio and video calls, facilitating virtual concerts, large-scale conferences, and other scenarios.
- Real-Time Status Synchronization: Supports orderly, high-frequency, and low-latency synchronization of status information such as position and avatar, and custom signaling for up to 10,000 people.
- Security and Compliance: Provides solutions such as cloud proxy for firewall environments, and capabilities such as data fencing for data compliance requirements in different regions.
- Cloud Services and Components: Meets more diverse usage scenarios by providing various services and components such as stream mixing and transcoding, audio moderation, video moderation, recording and screenshots.

#### Massive API Capabilities

- Basic Audio and Video: Provides capabilities for implementing 1v1 audio and video calls, multi-person audio and video calls, and multi-room audio and video calls.
- Advanced Video Processing: Provides capabilities such as layered encoding, super-resolution, low-light processing, background segmentation, basic AI Effects, capture rotation, screen sharing, watermarks, and screenshots.
- Advanced Audio Processing: Provides capabilities such as 3A, scenario-based AI noise reduction, voice changing & beautification & reverb, spatial audio, ear return, range audio and video, and voice detection.
- Value-Added Cloud Services: Provides capabilities such as stream mixing and transcoding, CDN relaying, low bitrate high quality, 10,000-person RTC co-hosting, ultra-low latency live streaming, real-time synchronization of 10,000 people's status, media stream input, and SIP interoperability.
- Quality Services: Provides capabilities such as pre-call device detection, real-time network detection, in-call quality insights, and full-link operations (Analytics Dashboard).
- Multiple Ecosystem Components: Provides components such as audio moderation, video moderation, advanced AI Effects filters and stickers, Super Board, recording and screenshots, and In-app chat (ZIM).


#### Global High Availability Service

- Deep Adaptation for Multiple Terminals and Platform Languages: Deeply adapted to various headphones, sound cards, cameras, and other peripherals, supporting 15,000+ terminal devices and IoT devices, compatible with 25+ languages and development platforms.
- High Availability in Complex Network Environments: Audio can resist up to 80% packet loss, video can resist 70% packet loss, achieves ultra-strong anti-jitter capability of 1000ms, and network bandwidth limitation is as low as 30kbps.
- Global Coverage of Massive Ordered Network MSDN: Provides 500+ nodes covering 212 countries with stable transmission networks. End-to-end latency is as low as 79ms, providing audio and video experience comparable to reality, with average long-distance end-to-end transmission latency of 300ms.
- Service High Availability Up to 99.99%: Provides a high-performance architecture that can accommodate tens of millions of high concurrency. Based on online scale, achieves second-level smooth scaling, while providing the Analytics Dashboard product to support real-time quality tracking, problem identification and resolution.
