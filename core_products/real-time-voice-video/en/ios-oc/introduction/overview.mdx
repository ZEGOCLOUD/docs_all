# Overview

---

Express Video is a real-time audio and video interaction service product that provides developers with easy access, high reliability, and multi-platform interoperability audio and video services. With an end-to-end average latency as low as 200 ms, industry-leading QoS strategies for weak network quality, and powerful 3A processing capabilities, it perfectly supports one-to-many, many-to-many real-time audio and video calls, live streaming, conferences, and other scenarios.

Express Video also provides developers with the ability to access audio and video services across all platforms with just 4 lines of code, enabling developers to build products and services with perfect audio and video experiences within 30 minutes.

For related concepts, please refer to [Glossary](/glossary/term-explanation).

## Product Advantages

**Intelligent and Realistic Image Quality**

- Deep Device Adaptation: Deep adaptation to various cameras online, achieving extremely low capture latency and high image quality.
- Ultra-high Image Quality: Supports up to 4K 60 fps ultra-high quality video.
- Proprietary Encoder: Self-developed optimized codec z264, which provides higher image quality under the same conditions, with extremely obvious improvements for complex scenes.
- AI Video Engine: Proprietary AI video engine supporting multi-fold super-resolution, low-light enhancement, background segmentation, frame interpolation, and other capabilities to comprehensively improve image quality.

**Immersive Sound Quality**

- Deep Device Adaptation: Deep adaptation to various microphones, sound cards, and other devices, achieving low-latency capture, low-latency ear return, and ultra-high fidelity sound quality.
- Rich Sound Effects: Optimized by professional audio team, with over 30 sound effect scenarios including voice changing, voice beautification, reverb, and virtual stereo.
- 3A and Contextual AI Noise Suppression: Proprietary 3A algorithm and industry's lightest AI noise suppression, achieving pure voice after noise and echo suppression with extremely low performance loss. Industry-first contextual AI noise suppression, real-time scene recognition, intelligently ensuring the comprehensive effect of noise suppression and sound quality.
- Range Audio: Combining distance, direction, transmission medium, and other factors, using professional algorithms to simulate realistic immersive audio and video call experience.

**Unlimited Features and Scenarios**

- Large-scale Co-hosting Interaction: No limit on the number of people in the room, supporting up to 10,000 people on mic for audio and video calls, facilitating virtual concerts, large-scale conferences, and other scenarios.
- Real-time State Synchronization: Supports orderly high-frequency and low-latency synchronization of state information such as position and avatar and custom signals for 10,000 people.
- Security and Compliance: Provides solutions such as cloud proxy for firewall environments, and capabilities such as data fencing for data compliance requirements in different regions.
- Cloud Services and Components: Meets more diverse usage scenarios, providing various services and components such as stream mixing and transcoding, audio moderation, video moderation, recording and screenshots.

**Massive Capability APIs**

- Basic Audio and Video: Provides capabilities for implementing 1v1 audio and video calls, multi-person audio and video calls, and multi-room audio and video calls.
- Advanced Video Processing: Provides capabilities such as layered encoding, super-resolution, low-light processing, background segmentation, basic AI effects, capture rotation, screen sharing, watermarks, and screenshots.
- Advanced Audio Processing: Provides capabilities such as 3A, contextual AI noise suppression, voice changing and beautification and reverb, spatial audio, ear return, range audio, voice detection, etc.
- Value-added Cloud Services: Provides capabilities such as stream mixing and transcoding, relay to CDN, low bitrate high definition, 10,000-person RTC co-hosting, ultra-low latency live streaming, real-time synchronization of 10,000 people's states, media stream input, SIP interoperability, etc.
- Quality Services: Provides capabilities such as pre-call device detection, real-time network detection, in-call quality insights, full-link operation and maintenance (Analytics Dashboard), etc.
- Various Ecosystem Components: Provides components such as audio moderation, video moderation, advanced AI effect filters and stickers, Super Board, recording and screenshots, in-app chat (ZIM), etc.


**Global High Availability Service**

- Deep Adaptation to Multiple Terminals and Platform Languages: Deep adaptation to various headphones, sound cards, cameras, and other peripherals, adapted to 15000+ terminal devices and IoT devices, compatible with 25+ languages and development platforms.
- High Availability in Complex Network Environments: Audio resists up to 80% packet loss, video resists 70% packet loss, achieves ultra-strong anti-jitter capability of 1000ms, and network bandwidth limitation is as low as 30kbps.
- Massive Global Coverage of MSDN Network: Provides 500+ nodes covering 212 countries with stable transmission network. End-to-end latency as low as 79ms, comparable to realistic audio and video experience, and average long-distance end-to-end transmission latency of 300ms.
- Up to 99.99% Service High Availability: Provides high-performance architecture that can accommodate tens of millions of high concurrency. Achieves second-level smooth scaling based on online traffic, while providing Analytics Dashboard products to support real-time quality tracking, problem location and resolution, etc.
