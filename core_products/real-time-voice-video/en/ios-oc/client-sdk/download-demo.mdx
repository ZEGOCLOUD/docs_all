## Live Audio Room

Live Audio Room refers to an online voice co-host virtual room. Each room has 4-8 microphone positions. The host chats on the microphone while streaming to other users in the room. Other listeners can enter the room to listen. The host can also invite listeners to come on stage for interaction. Different room types have different numbers of microphone positions and maximum number of listeners in the room.

- [Scenario Introduction](/live-audio-room-android-java/overview)
- [App Download](/live-audio-room-android-java/download-demo)

## Online Classroom Demo
The Online Classroom Demo is an online interactive classroom aPaaS solution provided by ZEGO. It modularly assembles the capabilities required for online classrooms, such as audio and video calls, whiteboard/files, cloud recording, real-time messages, and business backends. Developers do not need to separately integrate third-party SDKs and services, and can launch a dedicated interactive teaching platform in as fast as 15 minutes. The Online Classroom Demo not only covers teaching scenarios of multiple class types, but also supports flexible and scalable business functions based on guaranteeing real-time interactive classroom functions, seamlessly integrating with ZEGO's other business systems through standard interfaces, greatly reducing the development threshold.
