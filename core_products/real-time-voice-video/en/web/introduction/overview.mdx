# Overview

---

Video Call is a real-time audio and video interaction service product that provides developers with convenient access, high reliability, and multi-platform interoperability audio and video services. With an end-to-end average latency as low as 200 ms, industry-leading QoS strategies for weak network quality, combined with powerful 3A processing capabilities, it perfectly supports one-to-many, many-to-many real-time audio and video calls, live streaming, conferences, and other scenarios.

Video Call also provides developers with the ability to access audio and video services across all platforms in just 4 lines of code, enabling developers to build products and services with perfect audio and video experiences within 30 minutes.

For related concepts, please refer to [Terminology](/glossary/term-explanation).

## Product Advantages

#### Intelligent and Realistic Image Quality

- Deep Device Adaptation: Deeply adapted to various online cameras, achieving extremely low capture latency and high-quality image.
- Ultra-High Quality: Supports up to 4K 60 fps ultra-high quality video.
- Proprietary Encoder: Proprietary optimized codec z264 delivers higher image quality under the same conditions, with extremely significant improvements for complex scenes.
- AI Video Engine: Proprietary AI video engine supports multi-fold super-resolution, low-light enhancement, background segmentation, frame interpolation, and other capabilities, comprehensively improving image quality.

#### Immersive Audio Quality

- Deep Device Adaptation: Deeply adapted to various microphones, sound cards, and other devices, achieving low-latency capture, low-latency ear return, and ultra-high fidelity audio.
- Rich Sound Effects: Optimized by professional audio teams, with over 30 sound effect scenarios including voice changing, beautification, reverb, and virtual stereo.
- 3A and Scenario-Based AI Noise Reduction: Proprietary 3A algorithms and the industry's lightest AI noise reduction achieve pure voice with extremely low performance loss after noise and echo suppression. Industry-first scenario-based AI noise reduction recognizes scenarios in real-time, intelligently ensuring the comprehensive effect of noise reduction and audio quality.
- Range Audio: Combining distance, direction, transmission medium, etc., through professional algorithms, simulates realistic immersive audio and video call experiences.

#### Unlimited Features and Scenarios

- Large-Scale Co-hosting: No room limit, supports up to 10,000 people on mic for audio and video calls, assisting virtual concerts, large-scale conferences, and other scenarios.
- Real-Time Status Synchronization: Supports ordered, high-frequency, and low-latency synchronization of status information such as position and avatar, as well as custom signaling for 10,000 people.
- Security and Compliance: Provides solutions such as cloud proxy for firewall environments, and data fence capabilities for data compliance requirements in different regions.
- Cloud Services and Components: Meets more diverse features, providing various services and components such as stream mixing and transcoding, audio moderation, video moderation, recording screenshots, etc.

#### Massive Capability APIs

- Basic Audio and Video: Provides the ability to implement 1v1 audio and video calls, multi-person audio and video calls, and multi-room audio and video calls.
- Advanced Video Processing: Provides layered encoding, super-resolution, low-light processing, background segmentation, basic AI Effects, capture rotation, screen sharing, watermarks, and screenshots.
- Advanced Audio Processing: Provides 3A, scenario-based AI noise reduction, voice changing & beautification & reverb, spatial audio, ear return, range audio, voice detection, etc.
- Value-Added Cloud Services: Provides stream mixing and transcoding, relaying to CDN, low bitrate high definition, 10,000-person RTC co-hosting, ultra-low latency live streaming, 10,000-person real-time status synchronization, media stream input, SIP interoperability, etc.
- Quality Services: Provides pre-call device detection, real-time network detection, in-call quality insights, full-link operation and maintenance (Analytics Dashboard), etc.
- Multiple Ecosystem Components: Provides components such as audio moderation, video moderation, advanced AI Effects filters and stickers, Super Board, recording and screenshots, In-app chat (ZIM), etc.


#### Global High-Availability Service

- Deep Adaptation for Multiple Terminals and Platform Languages: Deeply adapted to various headphones, sound cards, cameras, and other peripherals, adapted to 15,000+ terminal devices and IoT devices, compatible with 25+ languages and development platforms.
- High Availability in Complex Network Environments: Audio resists up to 80% packet loss, video resists 70% packet loss, achieves ultra-strong anti-jitter capability of 1000ms, network bandwidth limit as low as 30kbps.
- Global Coverage of Massive Ordered Network MSDN: Provides 500+ nodes, covering 212 countries, with stable transmission network. End-to-end latency as low as 79ms, comparable to real-life audio and video experience, long-distance end-to-end transmission latency average 300ms.
- Up to 99.99% Service High Availability: Provides high-performance architecture that can accommodate tens of millions of high concurrency. Based on online scale, achieves second-level smooth scaling, while providing Analytics Dashboard products for real-time quality tracking, locating and solving problems.
