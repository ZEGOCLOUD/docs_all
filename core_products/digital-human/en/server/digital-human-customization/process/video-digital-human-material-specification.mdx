# Video Digital Human Material Specification

This document describes the video material specifications required for Video Digital Human customization.

## Video Material Requirements

If you are trying to record video materials yourself, please refer to the [Video Digital Human Shooting Guide](/aigc-digital-human-server/digital-human-customization/process/video-digital-human-shooting-guide).

After recording is complete and you are ready to provide materials for avatar creation, please ensure the video materials meet the following requirements:

|Category-150px | Description|
|----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Video specifications       | <ul> <li>Video duration: Total duration not less than 3 minutes, 5-10 minutes is optimal.</li> <li>Video size: Must be less than 20GB.</li> <li>Video specifications: Resolution of 1080P or above (4K recommended), frame rate of 25fps or above.</li> <li>Video ratio: Recommended 16:9 portrait or 9:16 landscape.</li> </ul>      |
| Frame composition       | <ul> <li>The person should occupy 1/2 to 2/3 of the video frame. The face and head area should not be less than 5% of the frame and must be clearly visible (see examples below).</li> <li>Supports half-body and full-body videos. The person must remain centered in the frame without obstructions.</li> </ul>                       |
| Person performance requirements   | <ul> <li>Front-facing and front-body posture. Head rotation should be within 30° of the screen angle, with a maximum supported angle of 60°.</li> <li>Natural standing/sitting posture without large movements such as bending over.</li> <li>Natural expressions without exaggeration (no loud laughter, frowning, etc.).</li> <li>Eyes should look straight ahead; avoid wandering gaze.</li> <li>Hand gestures and body movements should not block the head and neck area and should not extend beyond the frame.</li> </ul> |
| Frame background       | <ul> <li>Smooth, flat green screen background (can be chroma-keyed to replace background) or static scene background (background change not supported).</li> <li>Even, natural lighting. Not too dark or overexposed. Refer to the Video Digital Human Shooting Guide for lighting setup.</li> </ul>                                  |
| Person attire requirements   | <ul> <li>Person's attire must be clearly distinguishable from the background.</li> <li>In green screen environments, do not wear green or cyan-colored clothing.</li> <li>Reflective, translucent materials and plaid/stripe patterns are strictly prohibited in any background, as avatars cannot be customized with such materials.</li> <li>If the customer's clothing must be yellow, blue, or other colors similar to the green screen, the green screen can be replaced with a blue screen or other color. Please consult ZEGOCLOUD pre-sales for details.</li> </ul> |
