# Overview

---

Audio Call (Express Audio) is a real-time audio interactive service product that provides developers with easy-to-access, highly reliable, and cross-platform audio services. With an end-to-end average latency as low as 200 ms, industry-leading QoS strategies for weak network quality assurance, and powerful 3A processing capabilities, it perfectly supports one-to-many, many-to-many real-time audio calls, live streaming, conferences, and other scenarios.

Audio Call also provides developers with the ability to access audio services across all platforms with just 4 lines of code, enabling developers to build products and services with perfect audio experiences within 30 minutes.

## Product Advantages

#### Immersive Audio Quality

- Deep device adaptation: Deeply adapted to various microphones, sound cards, and other devices, achieving low-latency capture, low-latency ear return, and ultra-high fidelity audio quality.
- Rich audio effects: Optimized by professional audio teams, with over 30 audio effect scenarios including voice changing, voice beautification, reverb, and virtual stereo.
- 3A and scenario-based AI noise reduction: Proprietary 3A algorithms and lightweight AI noise reduction achieve pure human voice with extremely low performance loss after noise and echo suppression. Industry-first scenario-based AI noise reduction that intelligently identifies scenarios to ensure the comprehensive effect of noise reduction and audio quality in real time.
- Range audio and video: Combining distance, direction, transmission medium, etc., through professional algorithms, simulates realistic immersive audio and video call experiences.

#### Unlimited Gameplay and Scenarios

- Large-scale mic connection interaction: No room size limit, supporting up to 10,000 people for audio and video calls, facilitating virtual concerts, large-scale conferences, and other scenarios.
- Real-time status synchronization: Supports orderly, high-frequency, low-latency synchronization of status information such as position and avatar, and custom signaling for up to 10,000 people.
- Security and compliance: Provides solutions such as cloud proxy for firewall environments, and capabilities such as data fencing for data compliance requirements in different regions.
- Cloud services and components: Satisfies more diverse gameplay, providing various services and components such as mixing and transcoding, audio review, video review, recording screenshots, etc.

#### Massive Capability APIs

- Basic audio and video: Provides capabilities for 1v1 audio and video calls, multi-person audio and video calls, and multi-room audio and video calls.
- Advanced audio processing: Provides capabilities such as 3A, scenario-based AI noise reduction, voice changing, voice beautification, reverb, spatial audio effects, ear return, range audio and video, voice activity detection, etc.
- Value-added cloud services: Provides capabilities such as mixing and transcoding, CDN relay, 10,000-person RTC mic connection, ultra-low latency live streaming, real-time synchronization of 10,000 people's status, media stream input, SIP interoperability, etc.
- Quality services: Provides capabilities such as pre-call device testing, real-time network testing, in-call quality insights, full-link operations (Analytics Dashboard), etc.
- Various ecosystem components: Provides components such as audio review, Super Board, recording, In-app chat (ZIM), etc.

#### Global High-Availability Service

- Deep adaptation for multiple terminals and platform languages: Deeply adapted to various headphones, sound cards, cameras, and other peripherals, compatible with 15,000+ terminal devices and IoT devices, supporting 25+ languages and development platforms.
- High availability in complex network environments: Audio can withstand up to 80% packet loss, video can withstand 70% packet loss, achieves ultra-strong anti-jitter capability of 1000ms, and network bandwidth limitation is as low as 30k.
- Massive ordered network MSDN global coverage: Provides 500+ nodes covering 212 countries with stable transmission networks. End-to-end latency as low as 79ms, comparable to real-life audio and video experience, with average long-distance end-to-end transmission latency of 300ms.
- Up to 99.99% service high availability: Provides high-performance architecture that can accommodate tens of millions of high concurrency. Achieves second-level smooth scaling based on online scale, while providing Analytics Dashboard products to support real-time quality tracking, locating and solving problems, etc.
