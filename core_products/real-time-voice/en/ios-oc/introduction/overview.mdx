## What's the Voice Call

[ZEGOCLOUD's Voice Call](https://www.zegocloud.com/product/voice-call) is a service that empowers you to create high-quality and engaging voice call experiences for your web and mobile applications. With our SDK, you can easily integrate advanced features like spatial audio, intelligent voice processing, and even upgrade your service with add-ons that enable instant messaging and more.

Voice Call offers unparalleled flexibility, allowing you to tailor your audio experience to your exact needs. Whether you're building a multi-party conference call or a team voice chat for your online game, our platform has everything you need to bring your vision to life.

<Video src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/cWZ9v31Indk"/>



## When to use
To help you build a voice app that you exactly want, we've got you two types of SDKs, covering all phases and all your considerations: the SDK and the [UI Kit](https://www.zegocloud.com/docs/uikit/callkit-android/overview), these two get you all you need for building a voice app in two ways.

<Frame width="auto" height="auto">
  <img src="https://doc-media.zego.im/sdk-doc/Pics/A_VideoCallSDK/General/VideoCall_Overview_SDK_UIKit.jpeg" />
</Frame>


**Summary**:
If you are in the app feature development or feature upgrading phases and want the most control of your voice app, the Voice Call SDK is the Top Pick. 
When you are in the startup / trying phases, fund-seeking phase, or testing & POC phase and considering the budget issue, the [Call Kit](https://www.zegocloud.com/docs/uikit/callkit-android/overview) is your best choice to build an app in a short time.
