# Real-time Voice pricing

---

## Usage statistics

The usage of real-time audio services is calculated based on the duration during which users actually play audio streams.

- If users do not play audio or video streams in the room, no fees are charged.
- When an audio and video stream played by a user contains both video stream data and audio stream data, only the video duration is counted once, and the audio duration will not be calculated separately.

<Note title="Notes">
**Precision description:**
 - Duration usage precision is in seconds. When billing, the cumulative seconds within a billing cycle are converted into minutes for statistics. Any part of a minute is counted as one minute, and billing is ultimately based on the number of minutes.

**Playing stream description:**
 - Audio playing refers to users obtaining "audio data streams" through clients (or other terminals).
 - Calculated based on the duration from when the user starts playing the stream to when the user stops playing the stream.
</Note>
