# Product Features

- - -

## Communication Capabilities

### Basic Features

| Basic Feature-20%    | Feature Description-50%    | Application Scenario-30%    |
|-----|-----|-----|
| Audio Call  | Users can join the same Room and make audio calls. | <ul><li>1v1 audio call</li><li>Multi-person audio conference</li></ul> |
| Audio Live Streaming  | In the same Room, there are Hosts and Audiences. Hosts can publish audio live streaming, and Audiences in the Room can play the live stream. | <ul><li>Emotional FM</li><li>Live Audio Room</li></ul>  |
| [User Permission Control](/real-time-voice-cocos-creator/communication/using-token-authentication)  | Use Token to control user permissions, such as: specifying users can enter/leave the Room; specifying users to speak/mute; specifying users. | Video conference  |

### Advanced Features

| Advanced Feature-20%    | Feature Description-50%    | Application Scenario-30%    |
|-----|-----|-----|
| Live Co-hosting | Multiple Hosts can appear in the same Room, conducting live co-hosting on the same screen. | <ul><li>Multi-host co-hosting from different regions</li><li>Multi-person KTV singing</li><li>Showroom live streaming</li></ul>  |
| [Traffic Control](/real-time-voice-cocos-creator/communication/traffic-control)  | ZEGOCLOUD's industry-leading technology. The SDK dynamically adjusts the video publishing stream's bitrate, frame rate, resolution, and audio bitrate according to its own and the peer's current network environment, automatically adapting to network conditions and fluctuations to ensure smooth stream publishing. | All scenarios requiring high-quality real-time audio and video services |


## Audio Capabilities

### Basic Features

| Basic Feature-20%    | Feature Description-50%    | Application Scenario-30%    |
|-----|-----|-----|
| [Audio 3A Processing](/real-time-voice-cocos-creator/audio/audio-3a-processing)   | During real-time audio and video call or live streaming, 3A processing can be performed on audio to improve call or streaming quality and user experience.<ul><li>AEC (Acoustic Echo Cancellation): Filters collected audio data to reduce echo in the audio.</li><li>AGC (Automatic Gain Control): When enabled, the SDK can automatically adjust the microphone volume to adapt to near and far pickup and maintain a stable volume.</li><li>ANS (Automatic Noise Suppression): Identifies and eliminates background noise in the sound. After enabling this feature, the human voice becomes clearer.</li></ul> | All scenarios requiring high-quality real-time audio and video services |

### Advanced Features

| Advanced Feature-20%    | Feature Description-50%    | Application Scenario-30%    |
|-----|-----|-----|
| [Scenario-Based AI Noise Reduction](/real-time-voice-cocos-creator/audio/scenario-based-ai-noise-reduction) | Automatically recognizes different scenarios in real time, intelligently adjusts the AI noise reduction strategy to provide the optimal noise reduction and sound quality.<br />In call scenarios, all sounds except human voice are recognized and eliminated as noise.<br />In music scenarios, noise reduction adjusts automatically to restore music sound quality. | Voice chat room, conference, audio gaming and other 1v1 or multi-person audio and video call scenarios, as well as sound card, accompaniment, nearby music live streaming or online KTV scenarios |
