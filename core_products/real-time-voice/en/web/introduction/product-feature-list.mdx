## Communication Capabilities

### Basic Features

| Basic Features-20%    | Feature Description-50%    | Business Scenarios-30%    |
|-----|-----|-----|
| [Audio Call](/real-time-voice-web/quick-start/implementing-voice-call)   | Users join the same room and conduct audio calls. | <ul><li>1v1 voice call</li>  <li>Multi-person voice conference</li></ul> |
| [Audio Live Streaming](/real-time-voice-web/quick-start/implementing-voice-call)   | In the same room, including hosts and audience, hosts can conduct audio live streaming, and audience in the room can watch the live stream.| <ul><li>Emotional FM</li>  <li>Voice chat room</li></ul>  |
| [User Permission Control](/real-time-voice-web/communication/using-token-authentication)  | Use Token to control user permissions, such as: specifying users to enter/leave rooms; specifying users to speak/mute; specifying users. | Video conference  |
| Pre-call Detection | Before conducting audio/video calls or live streaming, perform device detection on cameras, microphones, monitors, etc., to ensure the normal operation of calls or live streaming.  | Normal call function detection  |
| [Call Quality Monitoring](/real-time-voice-web/communication/monitor-stream-quality) | Detect the quality of audio/video, such as resolution, frame rate, bitrate, sampling rate, and other multi-metric detection, to ensure stable quality. | Bank account opening, remote authentication, and other scenarios with high requirements and limitations on audio/video quality    |

### Advanced Features

| Advanced Features-20%    | Feature Description-50%    | Business Scenarios-30%    |
|-----|-----|-----|
| [Live Streaming Co-hosting](/real-time-voice-web/quick-start/implementing-voice-call)  | In one room, multiple hosts can appear and conduct on-screen co-hosting live streaming. | <ul><li>Multi-host co-hosting from different regions</li> <li>Multi-person KTV chorus</li><li>Showcase live streaming</li></ul>  |
| Push Multiple Streams Simultaneously  | One user can push multiple audio/video streams, such as sending the camera's video stream while sharing the screen. | Playing PPT during video conferences while seeing the speaker's screen |
| [Supplemental Enhancement Information (SEI)](/real-time-voice-web/communication/sei)  | Text information is packed with audio/video content and transmitted through the streaming media channel, to achieve precise synchronization between text data and audio/video content.  | <ul><li>Precise layout of video screens</li> <li>Remote lyrics synchronization</li> <li>Live quiz</li></ul> |
| [Flow Control](https://doc-zh.zego.im/faq/flowcontrol?product=ExpressVideo&platform=all)  | ZEGO industry-leading technology. The SDK dynamically adjusts video publishing bitrate, frame rate, resolution, and audio bitrate based on its own and the peer's current network environment status, automatically adapting to the current network environment and network fluctuations, thereby ensuring smooth video publishing.| All scenarios that require high-quality real-time audio/video services |
| [Cloud Proxy](/real-time-voice-web/communication/cloud-proxy) | By setting the SDK's cloud proxy interface, all traffic corresponding to the SDK is relayed through cloud proxy servers, to achieve communication with RTC and L3 (Ultra-low Latency Live Streaming).| Hospitals, government, company intranets, and other restricted network environments |
| [Geofencing](/real-time-voice-web/communication/geofencing) | Restrict audio/video and signaling data transmission to a certain region, to comply with regional data privacy and security regulations, that is, restrict access to audio/video services in a specific region. | Call scenarios  |
| [Game Voice](/real-time-voice-web/communication/range-audio)  | Mimic the real world, where people have different auditory experiences based on factors such as the direction and distance of sound, for example, the farther the distance, the smaller the sound. At the same time, grouping restrictions can be applied to people who can receive the audio source, for example, in a room, group discussions, different groups cannot hear each other. | <ul><li>Metaverse</li> <li>Same room, group communication or battles</li></ul> |

## Audio Capabilities

### Basic Features

| Basic Features-20%    | Feature Description-50%    | Business Scenarios-30%    |
|-----|-----|-----|
| [Sound Level Changes](/real-time-voice-web/audio/sound-level-spectrum)   | Sound level changes: the volume level of a specific stream. | <ul><li>Determine which user on mic is speaking, whether microphone, speaker, etc. are available</li> <li>Audio spectrum animation display, etc.</li></ul>  |
| [Headphone Monitor and Channel Setting](/real-time-voice-web/audio/headphone-monitor)        | <ul><li>Headphone acquisition monitoring: after inserting headphones (ordinary headphones or Bluetooth headphones) into the device, you can hear the sound collected by the device's microphone from the local headphone side.</li> <li>Dual channels: two audio channels. When hearing sound, you can determine the specific position of the sound source based on the phase difference between the left and right ears.</li></ul> |<ul><li>Showcase live streaming</li> <li>Emotional FM</li> <li>Music teaching and other professional scenarios</li></ul>|
| Audio 3A Processing  | During real-time audio/video calls or live streaming, 3A processing can be performed on audio to improve call or live streaming quality and user experience.<ul><li>AEC (Acoustic Echo Cancellation): Filter collected audio data to reduce echo in audio.</li><li>AGC (Automatic Gain Control): After enabling this feature, the SDK can automatically adjust microphone volume, adapt to near and far sound pickup, and keep volume stable.</li><li>ANS (Acoustic Noise Suppression): Identify background noise in sound and eliminate it. After enabling this feature, human voice can be clearer.</li></ul> | All scenarios that require high-quality real-time audio/video services |
| [Voice Changer/Reverb/Stereo](/real-time-voice-web/audio/audio-effects)   | <p>To increase fun and interactivity, users can use voice changers to play pranks, use reverb to enhance atmosphere, and use stereo to make sound more three-dimensional.</p><p>ZEGO Express SDK provides a variety of preset voice changer, reverb, reverb echo, and stereo effects. Developers can flexibly set the desired sound.</p> | <ul><li>Live streaming</li><li>Voice chat room</li><li>K song room</li><li>Anonymous social</li><li>Game entertainment</li><li>Role-playing</li></ul>  |
