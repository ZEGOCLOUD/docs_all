# Overview
---
Real-time Voice (Express Audio) is a real-time audio interactive service product that provides developers with convenient access, high reliability, and cross-platform audio services. With an end-to-end average latency as low as 200 ms, industry-leading QoS strategies for weak network quality assurance, and combined powerful 3A processing capabilities, it perfectly supports one-to-many, many-to-many real-time audio calls, live streaming, conferences, and other scenarios.

Real-time Voice also provides developers with the ability to access audio services across all platforms with just 4 lines of code, enabling developers to build products and services with perfect audio experiences within 30 minutes.

## Product Advantages

#### Immersive Audio Quality

- Deep device adaptation: Deeply adapted to various microphones, sound cards, and other devices, achieving low-latency capture, low-latency ear monitoring, and ultra-high fidelity audio quality.
- Rich audio effects: Optimized by professional audio teams, with over 30 audio effect scenarios including voice changing, beautification, reverb, and virtual stereo.
- 3A and scenario-based AI noise reduction: Proprietary 3A algorithms and lightweight AI noise reduction achieve pure human voice with extremely low performance loss after noise and echo suppression. Industry-first scenario-based AI noise reduction that intelligently recognizes scenarios to ensure comprehensive noise reduction and audio quality effects.
- Range audio and video: Combining distance, direction, transmission medium, etc., using professional algorithms to simulate realistic immersive audio and video call experiences.

#### Unlimited Gameplay and Scenarios

- Large-scale mic connection interaction: No room size limit, supporting up to 10,000 people in audio and video calls, facilitating virtual concerts, large-scale conferences, and other scenarios.
- Real-time status synchronization: Supports orderly, high-frequency, low-latency synchronization of status information such as position and avatar, as well as custom signaling for 10,000 people.
- Security and compliance: Provides solutions such as cloud proxy for firewall environments, and capabilities such as data geofencing for compliance requirements in different regions.
- Cloud services and components: To meet more diverse gameplay needs, provides various services and components such as stream mixing and transcoding, audio moderation, video moderation, recording, screenshots, and more.

#### Massive Capabilities API

- Basic audio and video: Provides capabilities for implementing 1v1 audio and video calls, multi-person audio and video calls, and multi-room audio and video calls.
- Advanced audio processing: Provides capabilities such as 3A, scenario-based AI noise reduction, voice changing, voice beautification, reverb, spatial audio effects, ear monitoring, range audio and video, voice activity detection, and more.
- Value-added cloud services: Provides capabilities such as stream mixing and transcoding, CDN relay, 10,000-person RTC mic connection, ultra-low latency live streaming, real-time synchronization of 10,000 people's status, media stream input, SIP interoperability, and more.
- Quality services: Provides capabilities such as pre-call device testing, real-time network testing, in-call quality insights, full-link operation and maintenance (Analytics Dashboard), and more.
- Various ecosystem components: Provides components such as audio moderation, Super Board, recording, in-app chat (ZIM), and more.

#### Global High-Availability Service

- Deep adaptation for multiple terminals and platform languages: Deeply adapted to various headphones, sound cards, cameras, and other peripherals, adapted to 15,000+ terminal devices and IoT devices, compatible with 25+ languages and development platforms.
- High availability in complex network environments: Audio can withstand up to 80% packet loss, video can withstand 70% packet loss, achieves super strong anti-jitter capability of 1000ms, with network bandwidth limitation as low as 30k.
- Global coverage of massive ordered network MSDN: Provides 500+ nodes covering 212 countries with stable transmission networks. End-to-end latency as low as 79ms, comparable to real-life audio and video experience, with average long-distance end-to-end transmission latency of 300ms.
- Service high availability of up to 99.99%: Provides high-performance architecture that can accommodate tens of millions of high concurrency. Based on online scale, achieves second-level smooth scaling, while providing Analytics Dashboard products for real-time quality tracking, problem location and resolution, etc.
