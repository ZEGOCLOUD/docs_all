## Communication Capabilities

### Basic Functions

| Basic Functions-20%    | Function Description-50%    | Business Scenarios-30%    |
|-----|-----|-----|
| [Audio Call](/real-time-voice-windows/quick-start/implementing-voice-call)   | Users join the same room and conduct audio calls. | <ul><li>1v1 voice call</li>  <li>Multi-person voice conference</li></ul> |
| [Audio Live Streaming](/real-time-voice-windows/quick-start/implementing-voice-call)   | In the same room, including a host and audience, the host can conduct audio live streaming, and the audience in the room can watch the live stream.| <ul><li>Emotional FM</li>  <li>Voice chat room</li></ul>  |
| [User Permission Control](/real-time-voice-windows/communication/using-token-authentication)  | Use Token to control user permissions, such as: specifying users can enter/exit rooms; specifying users to speak/mute; specifying users. | Video conference  |
| Pre-call Detection | Before conducting audio and video calls or live streaming, perform device detection on cameras, microphones, monitors, etc. to ensure the normal operation of calls or live streaming.  | Normal call function detection  |
| [Call Quality Monitoring](/real-time-voice-windows/communication/monitor-stream-quality) | Detect the quality of audio and video, such as resolution, frame rate, bitrate, sampling rate, and other multi-indicator detection to ensure stable quality. | Bank account opening, remote appraisal, and other scenarios with high requirements and limitations on audio and video quality    |
| [Network Speed Test](/real-time-voice-windows/communication/testing-network) | Before users publish/play streams, detect the upstream and downstream network speeds to determine the suitable bitrate of audio and video streams for publishing/playing in the current network environment.  | Call scenarios, education scenarios, live streaming scenarios  |

| [Mass-scale Range Audio and Video](/real-time-voice-windows/communication/mass-scale-range-audio-video) | ZEGO industry-leading technology. | [Real-time Multi-user Status Sync](/real-time-voice-windows/communication/sync-users-status) | ZEGO industry-leading technology. Provides an ordered, high-frequency, low-latency, large-scale status synchronization service, helping developers quickly implement real-time information synchronization capabilities such as player positions, actions, and appearances in virtual gameplay, while supporting 10,000 users online simultaneously in a single scene. | Virtual offices, virtual exhibitions, virtual social networking, virtual KTV, and other metaverse scenarios, as well as general scenarios that require ultra-high frequency, low latency, and large-scale synchronization of information or control commands |

### Basic Functions

|-----|-----|-----|
| [Stream Mixing](/real-time-voice-windows/live-streaming/stream-mixing)      | Mix multiple users' multiple video streams into one stream, so that you only need to play one stream to see the screens of all members in the room and hear the sounds of all members in the room. | Multi-person call host co-hosting  |
