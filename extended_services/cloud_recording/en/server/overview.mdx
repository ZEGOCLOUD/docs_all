# API overview

---

ZEGOCLOUD's Cloud Recording Server provides the following APIs, and the following are their reference documents: 

|API reference doc|Description| Default QPS |
|-|-|-|
|[Start recording](./start-record.mdx)| Starts cloud recording. | 50 times/second |
|[Stop recording](./stop-record.mdx)| Stops cloud recording. | 50 times/second |
|[Update the mixed-stream layout](./update-layout.mdx)|Updates the mixed-stream layout settings at any time during the recording. | 10 times/second                    |
|[Update the whiteboard](./update-whiteboard.mdx)| Updates the whiteboard at any time during the recording. | 10 times/second         |
|[Query recording status](./describe-record-status.mdx)| Queries the status of a recording task after recording starts. | 10 times/second                       |
|[Query the recording task list](./describe-tasks.mdx)| Queries the list of recording tasks after recording starts. | 10 times/second                       |
|[Pause recording](./pause-record.mdx)| Pauses a task of recording audio streams, video streams, and whiteboards in a room. | 10 times/second                       |
|[Resume recording](./resume-record.mdx)| Resumes a task of recording audio streams, video streams, and whiteboards in a room. | 10 times/second                       |
|[Take a Snapshot](./take-snapshot.mdx)| Takes a snapshot of the mixed stream. | 10 times/second                       |
|[Recording status callback](./recording-status-callback.mdx)| Gets the task status event. You need to provide a callback URL when enabling the Cloud Recording service. | - |
