# Performance Data

- - -


#### Test Host Configuration

- Alibaba Cloud ECS instance: ecs.c6.2xlarge
- 8 vCPU cores, 16GB RAM
- Disk I/O: 106 MB/s
- Bandwidth: 100 Mbps

#### Test Conditions

- Publish 1 single stream with resolution 360 * 180, frame rate 15 fps, and bitrate 300 kbps to a room, and perform single-stream recording.
- Publish 2 single streams with resolution 360 * 180, frame rate 15 fps, and bitrate 300 kbps to a room, and perform mix-stream recording. The mix output video has resolution 640 * 360, frame rate 15 fps, and bitrate 600 kbps.

#### Concurrent Recording Performance

| Recording Mode | Test Result | Recommendation |
|-|-|-|
| Audio/Video Single-Stream Recording | Concurrently recording 350 rooms causes stuttering in recorded video files. | Recommend concurrent recording of up to 300 rooms. |
| 2-Stream Audio/Video Mix-Stream Recording | Concurrently recording 45 rooms with 75% CPU usage. | Recommend concurrent recording of up to 40 rooms. |
| 2-Stream Audio-Only Mix-Stream Recording | Concurrently recording 230 rooms with 85% CPU usage. | Recommend concurrent recording of up to 200 rooms. |

#### Conclusion

Based on the test host above:

- The performance bottleneck for single-stream recording is primarily bandwidth. For more concurrent single-stream recordings, higher bandwidth is needed. Disk I/O usage should also be monitored.
- The performance bottleneck for mix-stream recording is primarily CPU. For more concurrent mix-stream recordings, a higher-performance CPU is needed.
